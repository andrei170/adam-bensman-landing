How the cohort runs

Eight weeks. Four modules. Your operation on the table.

1

Submit the four-minute application

Current role, current monthly revenue, team size, closing ratio, primary bottleneck, and current training stack. The application is a fit-check, not a gatekeeper. No setter call. No back-and-forth. Submit, get routed to the cohort calendar, pick a cycle, enrol.

2

Week 1 live orientation - your operation mapped

First call with Adam and the cohort. You bring your current numbers: closing ratio, team size, rep ramp time, monthly revenue, primary bottleneck. Adam maps where the ceiling is. The four-module sequence is calibrated to the cohort's operations.

3

Weekly live group call, weeks 1-8

60-90 minutes per call. One module, one focus. Adam on the call. Biweekly office hours block (90 minutes) for operation-specific questions. Private community channel for the cohort for async support across the eight weeks.

4

Week 2 operation walk - your numbers on camera

Cohort members who opt in share their branch P&L, team structure, or recruiting funnel with Adam on the call for a live operation review. This is the install layer that the courses cannot provide. Your bottleneck gets walked, not theorised.

5

Post-cohort routing to the right next step

Cohort grads who qualify get routed to the Roofing STRONG Alliance application as the natural high-touch back-end. Grads in the $5MM-$13MM band get routed to 1-1 consulting. The cohort qualifies; it does not replace the alliance or the consulting tier.
