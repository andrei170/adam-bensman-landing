Who This Is For

If your rep ramp or your roofing operation is stuck, the bottleneck is one of four things.

01

The Elite Roofing Sales Rep

Currently a D2D or storm-damage rep at a small-to-mid roofing company. Personal commission $200K-$500K. No team yet, or 1-2 direct reports building under you. The cohort walks your role in week 2 and names the leadership bottleneck so you stop guessing what the next move is.

02

The Sub-$5M Roofing Company Owner

You own a roofing company at $1MM-$5MM annual revenue. Team of 5-15 producers (canvassers, closers, project managers, supplementers). The ceiling has held for the last 6-12 months. The cohort surfaces a recruiting SOP rewrite and a branch P and L rewrite that fits your operation.

03

The Storm Specialist Adding Retail

Strong on insurance work, weaker on retail. Revenue volatile by season and geography. You want a retail playbook to flatten the curve. The cohort gives you the four playbooks before you launch the retail funnel.

The Work

Four modules. Eight weeks.

No video-on-demand library. The whole thing is built around weekly live group calls and scheduled office hours.

Module One · Weeks 1-2

D2D + Storm-Damage Closing Systems

By the end of week 2, you have the pitch system, the objection map, and the closing cadence that fits your role, your neighbourhood, and your storm versus retail mix.

The pitch system I have run for 13 years on the doors. The 10-Second-Pitch flagship cut walked at module depth. Objection handling for insurance work versus retail. How the Roof Strategist Sales System operationalises across all 50 states. Your specific doors go on the table. The pitch and the objection bank get pressure-tested on the live call.

Module Two · Weeks 3-4

Recruiting + Retention

By the end of week 4, your sourcing channels, vetting bar, and 90-day retention SOP are written, and your existing team has a hold-or-coach call on each name.

Why reps ghost at month-3, and the SOP that holds them. The Build Your Dream Team material walked live. The retention conversation script. The post-launch rep review at week 6. How to read first-30-days door data and predict month-6 churn. Your specific team goes on the underwriting table. The hold-or-coach call gets documented.

Module Three · Weeks 5-6

Branch P and L + Ops

By the end of week 6, your roofing branch P and L map, KPI cadence, and weekly operations rhythm are locked, with the next 1-2 hires scoped.

The branch KPIs I track every week on every closer. The P and L map for a sub-$5M roofing branch and the same map for a $5MM-$13MM company. When to add a team lead versus when to stay flat. Office hours across timezones. Your operations doc gets the audit on the live call. The next 90-day plan gets written.

Module Four · Weeks 7-8

Personal-Brand + Content Compounding

By the end of week 8, you have a content cadence, a long-form authority lane, and a recruiting-flywheel system that compounds off your own brand.

How The STRONG Roofer YouTube channel and the Roof Strategist Podcast compounded the Sales System across 50 states, Canada, Australia, and Sweden. The content cadence I keep and the one I cut. How to build a credible long-form authority lane on your own scale, even with a 500-follower starting point. Your content plan gets the live audit. The next 30 days of content get scheduled.

Live group call once a week with Adam. Two scheduled office-hours blocks per month for 1:1 questions. Private community channel for the 8 weeks. Templates, scripts, SOPs, recruiting docs, and branch P and L worksheets handed off as you complete each module.

FAQ

Common questions, before applying.

How is this different from the Roof Strategist courses?

The Marketing Battle Pack, Complete Sales Strategy, and Build Your Dream Team are the productised course tier. The cohort is the install version. The courses teach you what to look for; the cohort walks your specific role, your specific neighbourhood, your specific branch on a live group call. Many cohort members have run the Sales System on doors for a year first and come in already knowing the vocabulary.

Is this the Roofing STRONG Alliance?

No. The cohort is sales operations and team-build training on Adam's owned funnel. The Roofing STRONG Alliance by TAMKO is the application-only contractor community at strongbytamko.com, with membership free for TAMKO Edge contractors. The cohort grads who qualify route into the alliance application as the natural high-touch back-end. The cohort feeds the alliance; the cohort does not replace it.

How fast will I see results?

Most cohort members name their primary bottleneck in week 1 and have the first SOP rewritten by week 3. The recruiting or retention rewrite lands by week 6. The full branch P and L map lands by week 8. Results vary, dependent on your current role, the time you invest in office hours, and your team quality.

Is this a course?

No. It is mentorship. Weekly live group calls with Adam, scheduled office-hours blocks for 1:1 questions, private community channel for the 8 weeks. There is no video-on-demand library to fall behind on.

What is the time commitment?

3-4 hours per week for 8 weeks. One live group call per week (60-90 min). Office hours optional. The work between calls is your real role or operation, not homework.

What is the investment?

Two tiers in the mid-tier mentorship range. Standard tier covers the 8-week cohort, weekly group calls, office hours, and templates. Standard-plus-operating-review tier adds a 1:1 review of your existing role or operation and three follow-up 1:1 calls in the 90 days post-cohort. Hint range, not an invoice. The application surfaces exact pricing for the cycle you apply to.

What if I am still a rep, not an owner?

The elite rep is one of the three primary avatars. Week 1 starts at sales-floor cadence and weekly closing metrics. Week 8 ends at a 90-day plan for the team-build move you want next.

What if I already run a $5M-$13M roofing company?

The cohort handles the scaling case. Week 5-6 walks branch P and L for a $5MM-$13MM operation. Week 7-8 walks the personal-brand-as-leverage layer that compounds the recruiting flywheel. The standard-plus-operating-review tier adds a 1:1 audit of your existing branch and three follow-up 1:1 calls post-cohort.

How does this work with Adam's 1-1 consulting?

The cohort is the productised mid-tier. If you want continued mentorship after the cohort, that route is the 1-1 consulting. If you want the high-touch contractor community, that route is the Roofing STRONG Alliance application at strongbytamko.com. The cohort feeds both; it does not replace either.

Refund policy?

Yes. Complete the first two weeks, decide it is not for you, full refund within 14 days of cohort kickoff. No questions. The same 30-day money-back guarantee Adam runs on the Marketing Battle Pack applies to the cohort.

Why do I have to apply?

You do not have to. The application form is four minutes and it just helps size cohorts and route the office-hours topics to what is actually on members' desks this cycle. Submit the form, you get straight to enrolment, no setter calls, no back-and-forth.
